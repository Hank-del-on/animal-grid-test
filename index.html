Some animals

A ferret is a small, carnivorous mammal that belongs to the Mustelidae family. They are known for their long, slender bodies and curious nature. Ferrets are popular pets due to their playful and energetic personalities. They are also used for hunting purposes, as they have a strong hunting instinct and are skilled at catching rodents. Ferrets are social animals and thrive in environments with plenty of mental and physical stimulation.
